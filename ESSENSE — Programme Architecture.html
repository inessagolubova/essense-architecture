FOUNDATION
What ESSENSE
is for.

Essense Business School exists to help us re-sense the meaning of business and bring business back in tune with life, so it becomes a life-value creating force. Life-value is the value a business creates that supports the life of the planet and all its life forms, now and in the future.

"In tune with life" means a business designed by a human who feels like a part of the living ecosystem. Naturally, their creation shall stay human, live, subject to natural limits, cycles, seasons, and place. As a living organism, the business interacts with and creates value for other elements of the ecosystem — as a whole, not only to sustain itself.

This is only possible if and when the creator is whole and free — inner clarity and coherence create agency and responsibility. So we start with the creator — enable them to re-sense and re-design their business to generate life.

VALUE PROPOSITION
"Build a business in tune with life."

We inspire leaders to shift how they make meaning — their inner operating system that in turn shapes what they notice, value, and how they relate to the world.

As that capacity grows, inner clarity becomes outer coherence — across strategy, decision-making, culture, operating rhythm, and naturally, bottomline.

Guided by life-value, not abstract growth, leaders design businesses that support the whole living systems we depend on — both human and non-human forms of life.

Supported by a shared community of teachers, peers, and alumni, they build businesses they do not need to escape.

WHAT IS SUCCESS?
How we know
we are thriving.
A Sticky Place
For participants, ESSENSE has become a place of life learning — abundant in opportunities that stay "at par with and ahead of" their evolving capacity and agency for life and business. A non-conventional curriculum, delivered in various formats and various learning paths that deepen and broaden their human selves and their businesses.
A Peer-Learning Place
Within cohorts, between cohorts and individuals — authentic and sound relationships between generations and lineages; respectful and liberating towards one another. Participants easily form learning groups, join each other's projects, help each other, and contribute to a joint body of knowledge.
A Place of Learning Innovation
In business education and in business practice — a living innovation bank, with ideas, experimentation protocols, testing results and action research.
A Sound Economic Foundation
Initial equity investments recuperated within 3–5 years. Medium operational profit margin. A large share of profit re-invested in years 1–4, and a smaller share invested into a cause. From year 3–4, a fund to support alumni's cause-initiatives.
A Community of Choice
ESSENSE is a learning community of choice for world-class inspirational teachers and practitioners: bold vision, honest delivery, authentic culture, inspired students, and fair contribution and profit-sharing agreements.
A Living Ecosystem
ESSENSE is designed as a living ecosystem of learning and community life — where learning, community, knowledge and spaces continuously nourish one another. Programs feed community. Community feeds knowledge creation. Knowledge feeds learning technologies. Physical spaces anchor the ecosystem in the world.
GOVERNANCE OF THE ECOSYSTEM
Three complementary
roles.
CO-FOUNDER 1
The Face & Voice
of ESSENSE
Holds the soul, voice and image of ESSENSE — and defines the direction in which the field moves.
  • Face & Voice — Interviews, podcasts, public talks, texts, manifestos and storytelling of the brand. How ESSENSE sounds and appears in the world.
  • Art Director — Defines the visual and aesthetic code: visual language, atmosphere, how programmes, media, materials and retreat spaces look and feel.
  • Producer — Initiates and produces key projects and formats (programmes, retreats, podcasts, special projects), connecting people, themes and contexts.
CO-FOUNDER 2
Academic Core &
Learning Experience
The architect of the academic dimension and the quality of transformation participants receive. Holds the intellectual and pedagogical integrity of ESSENSE — ensuring the ecosystem is not only inspiring and meaningful but also structurally sound and professionally delivered.
  • Academic framework & methodology — Defines conceptual frameworks, curricula, intellectual depth and coherence of learning journeys. Ensures everything remains methodologically sound.
  • Learning experience — Designs how participants experience the journey: module structures, rhythm, interaction formats, integration practices, reflection and support.
  • Quality standards & evaluation — Defines what constitutes a "high-quality ESSENSE experience"; establishes evaluation tools (feedback, NPS, qualitative transformation stories) and improves programmes based on real data.
  • Faculty & learning stewards — Selects and develops the teachers and facilitators delivering programmes, ensuring alignment with the academic core and ESSENSE approach.
CEO & ECOSYSTEM ARCHITECT
Architect of
the Ecosystem
The third center — architect of the ecosystem and its operational form. Holds form, structure and viability, allowing the field and academic depth of the co-founders to breathe freely.
  • Ecosystem architecture — Designs the overall map of ESSENSE: how different "biomes" (school, stewardship, family, corporate, media, community) connect; how participants move between them; how field, academic architecture and business model interact.
  • Business model & sustainability — Builds a financially sustainable 3–5 year model: revenue streams, cost structures, investments and growth horizons. Ensures depth is not consumed by operational chaos or financial stress.
  • Operating system — Creates processes, roles, rhythms and reporting systems. Ensures programmes, partnerships and content launch predictably and sustainably.
  • Embedding partnerships — Receives relationships initiated by co-founders and turns them into sustainable formats, agreements and operational processes — strengthening the field and academic core.
  • Investor Acquisition & Fundraising — Mapping: impact/regen funds, HNW families, aligned VCs (£150k–£1M rounds). Outreach: warm intros, events, pitch decks with life-value metrics. Deal structuring: equity/debt/stewardship models. Post-investment: reporting, alignment with values.
